Guidelines overview
General linking guidelines
ExtractSets
  • If there are multiple instances of an ExtractSet in the contract, this ExtractSet needs to be created and referenced as many times as needed.
Extracts
  • Consistency is crucial for machine learning performance. Consistency means:
    • Link a certain piece of information in the same way every time.
    • Always link information when it is present in the document. In particular, link every instance of an Extract in the contract and not only some of them.
  • Link short snippets of text; keep in mind that the text before and after the linked snippet is also considered by the algorithm.
  • Extract value should be a reflection of what is in the text. Don't draw conclusions or make inferences. Think as though you just have this snippet in front of you.
  • Avoid linking related items from two different pages as much as possible. Example: Let’s assume the contact address appears in two places. One of them is older and contains complete information, while the other is newer, has the same information except that it doesn't have the zip code. Either link the old one with complete information or the new one without the zip code. Don’t link the address from the new one and the zip code from the older one.
